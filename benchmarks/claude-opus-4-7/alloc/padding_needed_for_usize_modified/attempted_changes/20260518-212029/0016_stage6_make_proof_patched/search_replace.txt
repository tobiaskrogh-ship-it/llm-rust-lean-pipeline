/-! ## Extraction-infidelity witnesses at `align = 0`

These two `private theorem`s are *machine-checked* (no `sorry`). They pin the
exact reason the `align = 0` sub-case of `padding_needed_for_non_power_of_two`
cannot close, so a future pass does not have to re-derive it: the obstruction
is a provably-false goal caused by upstream extraction strictness, **not** a
missing prelude lemma to hunt for. -/

/-- Lifted from the in-proof `hreduce`: at `align = 0` the *whole* extracted
    `padding_needed_for` panics. `is_power_of_two_usize 0` underflows at
    `0 -? 1` (see `ipo2_zero`) because the Hax model of Rust's short-circuit
    `&&` is strict (`&&?` is `rust_primitives.hax.logical_op.and = pure (a &&
    b)`, a strict `Bool` function — verified in
    `Hax/rust_primitives/hax/logical_op.lean:11,25` — and the surrounding
    `do`-block hoists `(← (n -? 1))` unconditionally). The panic propagates
    through the monadic bind in `padding_needed_for`. Real Rust short-circuits
    `0 != 0` and returns `usize::MAX`. -/
private theorem padding_needed_for_zero_panics (size : usize) :
    padding_needed_for_usize.padding_needed_for size (0 : usize)
      = RustM.fail Error.integerOverflow := by
  unfold padding_needed_for_usize.padding_needed_for
  rw [ipo2_zero, rustM_fail_bind]

/-- Machine-checked refutation of the obligation's conclusion at `align = 0`:
    `decide` evaluates the decidable `RustM` (`= ExceptT Error Option`)
    equality and proves it false. So the `align = 0` sub-case is not a *hard*
    goal awaiting a clever tactic — it is a *provably false* goal, with no
    contradictory hypothesis available (`IsPow2 0` is itself false, so the
    obligation's `h : ¬ IsPow2 align` specialises to a true, leverage-free
    fact). The only unblock is upstream (a short-circuiting encoding of Rust
    `&&` in the Hax extraction) or restricting the obligation to
    `align ≠ 0`. -/
private theorem padding_needed_for_zero_ne_max (size : usize) :
    padding_needed_for_usize.padding_needed_for size (0 : usize)
      ≠ RustM.ok (18446744073709551615 : usize) := by
  rw [padding_needed_for_zero_panics]
  decide
