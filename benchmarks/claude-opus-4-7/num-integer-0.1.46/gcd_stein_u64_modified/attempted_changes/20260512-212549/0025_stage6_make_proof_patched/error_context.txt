Strengthen tzInv to the multiplicative form y * 2^count ≤ x.  The weaker form `count < 64` is not preserved by the body (count' = count + 1 could equal 64).  The multiplicative form derives `count < 64` as a consequence (2^count ≤ x < 2^64), and is preserved exactly because the body halves y while doubling 2^count.
