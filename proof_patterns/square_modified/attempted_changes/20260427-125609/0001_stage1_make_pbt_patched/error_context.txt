Six of the eight existing tests (square_is_monotone, square_of_nonzero_is_nonzero, square_is_strictly_monotone, square_preserves_parity, square_is_quadratic_residue_mod4, square_is_non_contracting) are derived mathematical facts about multiplication, not independent contract clauses. They must be removed to leave only the two genuine contract tests: the postcondition and the overflow-panic failure condition.
