The cbrt_cube_le_x theorem's `all_goals sorry` is too coarse — it gives up on every remaining VC without first trying standard decision procedures. Adding `all_goals try (first | rfl | trivial | decide | omega | simp_all)` before the final sorry attempts to discharge any VC that happens to reduce to a Presburger or propositional fact, leaving only the genuinely cubic / Newton-monovariant ones.
