  -- Substantive attempt. Unfold the function body and let `mvcgen`
  -- generate verification conditions for the 11-iteration `Tuple4` loop.
  -- This exposes the per-step VC structure.

  -- Try the generic VC generator; we know it cannot close the loop
  -- without an explicit invariant, but we record what it produces.
  -- Stuck at: the auto-generated VC for the inner `while_loop` body, which
  -- branches on `(x >>> s) Ōēź b` and requires the Hacker's-Delight cubic
  -- invariant

  -- the `then` branch (where y is incremented and y2 is updated).
  -- None of this is mechanizable by `omega` (it lacks `^3`) nor by
  -- `bv_decide` (it cannot see through `while_loop`'s partial-fixpoint).
  --

  --       <step relation>

  -- the step lemma at each fold. Lemma is classical (Hacker's-Delight
